User Manual

1Drop a Pin
Click anywhere on the map to place an analysis pin, or type a location name in the search bar. A dialog will ask you to name the pin (e.g. "Business Bay"). You can add multiple pins to compare several areas in one run. Drag a pin to reposition it.
2Set the Radius
The Radius field controls how far around each pin we pull restaurant data (1–30 km). All Talabat delivery areas whose centre falls within that circle are included. Smaller radius = faster run, more targeted data. Start with 10 km for a typical kitchen park coverage zone.
3Just Landed (optional)
Toggle Just Landed only to restrict results to restaurants Talabat has flagged as newly added. Useful for tracking new entrants in a target area without the noise of the full incumbent list. The result card will show a separate Just Landed count.
4Run the Analysis
Click Run Whitespace Analysis. The job runs in the background — you can close the browser and come back. The status card in the sidebar shows live progress: current area, page X / Y, and vendor count. An analysis covering 9 areas typically takes 25–40 minutes.
5Download Results
When complete, click Download Excel Report. The file has three sheets:
  • Matrix — brands as rows, your pins as columns, ✓ where the brand is present. Includes legal name, phone, Google Maps link, and KP tenant flag.
  • Raw — every individual branch with full detail including the unique namaa_id (NBT-XXXXXXX), coordinates, rating, delivery time, and is_new flag.
  • Facilities — KP facility metadata used in the run.
6Interactive Vendor Map
After an analysis completes, two map buttons appear in the top-right:
  • Heat — density heatmap with a Talabat orange gradient. Dark = sparse, gold = dense hotspot.
  • Vendors — individual coloured dots, one per restaurant, colour-coded by cuisine type. A legend appears bottom-right. Click any dot to open a popup with the brand name, rating, review count, distance from pin, and a direct View on Talabat → link.
7Results Archive
Click Archive in the header to open your results warehouse. Every completed analysis is saved here permanently with its pin names, radius, timestamp, brand count, record count, and unique SKU count. From any past result you can:
  • Load on Map — instantly re-plot that result's vendor layer without re-running.
  • Excel — re-download the full report at any time.
  • Compare — tick any two results and click Compare to see a diff: new brands that entered the market, brands that left, and the retained overlap. Uses the stable namaa_id for accurate matching.
8KP Facilities Layer
The KP Facilities toggle in the map shows all Kitchen Park locations. ● Pulsing green = Live. ◌ Spinning dashed = Coming Soon. Hover any marker for a Street View photo. Click a marker and hit Analyse this Location to instantly drop a pin at that facility.
9Understanding namaa_id
Every branch row in the Excel carries a stable unique identifier in the format NBT-0012345 (NAMAA Branch Talabat). Use this to track the same branch across multiple analysis runs, power the Archive compare feature, and build a longitudinal market database over time.
10Coverage Warning
If a pin is placed in a desert or uncovered area (no Talabat delivery zones within the chosen radius), the tool will show a ⚠ Coverage Warning banner instead of silently returning 0 results. Move the pin to an urban area or increase the radius and re-run.
